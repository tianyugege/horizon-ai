## [Visualization of 11M Scientific Papers by Semantic Similarity and Time](https://www.reddit.com/r/MachineLearning/comments/1ujn3u5/a_map_of_the_latest_11_million_papers_split_by/) ⭐️ 8.0/10

A new tool called The Global Research Space visualizes 11 million scientific papers by encoding titles and abstracts using SPECTER 2 embeddings and projecting them into 2D with UMAP. It supports semantic and keyword queries, time slicing, and analytics on authors and institutions, with daily updates. This tool significantly enhances the ability of researchers and institutions to explore vast scientific literature efficiently, revealing macro trends and enabling better discovery of relevant work. It addresses the challenge of keeping up with the rapidly growing volume of published papers. The project uses SPECTER 2 for embedding scientific texts and UMAP for dimensionality reduction, combined with Voronoi diagrams to label dense clusters. It includes a sliding time feature and an analytics layer ranking authors and institutions, but may be limited by embedding accuracy and visualization scalability.

reddit · r/MachineLearning · /u/icannotchangethename · Jun 30, 11:55

**Background**: Scientific literature is growing rapidly, making it difficult for researchers to keep track of new developments. Embedding models like SPECTER 2 convert text into numerical vectors capturing semantic meaning, while dimensionality reduction techniques like UMAP reduce these vectors to two dimensions for visualization. Voronoi diagrams partition the visual space to highlight clusters of related papers.
